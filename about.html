About Us

At Hand to Heart, we believe in the transformative power of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) to create positive change.

With a commitment to making a genuine difference, we dedicate ourselves to connecting institutions with the most deserving causes and communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve as a bridge between companies and Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs), facilitating impactful initiatives that leave a lasting impact. .

Fostering meaningful CSR initiatives

We are dedicated to facilitating impactful partnerships between companies and NGOs for the greater good.

Our goal is to streamline the process of corporate social responsibility, ensuring that your contributions make a significant impact on the lives of those who need it the most and bridge the gap between corporate entities and social responsibility by creating customized, transparent, and community-centric CSR solutions.

Our Approach

Needs Assessment: We conduct thorough assessments to identify the most pressing needs within communities, allowing for targeted and effective CSR programs.
Strategic Partnerships: Through our extensive network, we establish partnerships with NGOs and local organizations, maximizing the reach and impact of your CSR initiatives.
Transparent Reporting: Our commitment to transparency includes regular reporting on the progress and outcomes of your CSR projects, providing tangible evidence of the positive change achieved.
Community-Centric Approach: Hand to Heart is committed to empowering local communities. By focusing on grassroots initiatives, we ensure that the benefits of CSR projects directly reach the people who need them the most.
Strategic Partnership Building: We leverage our extensive network to connect companies and NGOs with shared interests, fostering collaborations that enhance the overall effectiveness and reach of CSR efforts.

Why Hand to Heart

Tailored Solutions: We understand that each company and NGO has unique values and goals. As Hand to Heart, we collaborate with partners to design CSR projects that align seamlessly with their mission, ensuring a purposeful and authentic contribution to society.
Expertise: Our team brings a wealth of experience in CSR project management, ensuring the success of your initiatives.
Community Empowerment: At Hand to Heart, we prioritize grassroots initiatives to ensure that the benefits of CSR projects directly reach the people who need them the most. By empowering local communities, we create sustainable, long-term impact.
Holistic Approach: We address societal needs comprehensively, ensuring that your contributions create sustainable and positive change.
Efficiency: Streamlined processes enable a seamless integration of CSR into your company's mission and values.

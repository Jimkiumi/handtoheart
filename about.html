about Chrity

We show our support to this kids and to those who take care of them by comming through when we can and giving the little support that God has enabled us to, so at HandtoHeart, we are driven by a simple yet profound belief: that everyone deserves a chance to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Founded with the mission to extend a helping hand to those in need, we are a voluntary services organization committed to making a tangible difference in the lives of the less fortunate.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to uplift and empower individuals and communities facing adversity through compassionate support and sustainable initiatives. Whether it's providing essential resources such as food, shelter, and healthcare, or offering educational opportunities and vocational training, we strive to address the root causes of poverty and inequality.

What We do

Through the dedication of our volunteers, the generosity of our donors, and the unwavering support of our partners, we work tirelessly to offer a range of services and programs tailored to meet the diverse needs of those we serve. From distributing emergency aid during times of crisis to implementing long-term development projects aimed at fostering self-sufficiency, our efforts are guided by a commitment to creating lasting positive change.

Our Impact

Over the years, we have witnessed firsthand the transformative power of kindness and solidarity. From helping individuals overcome personal challenges to strengthening the resilience of entire communities, each success story fuels our determination to continue our work with even greater passion and purpose.

Get Involved

Join us in our mission to build a more compassionate and equitable world. Whether you're interested in volunteering your time and skills, making a donation to support our programs, or simply spreading the word about our cause, there are countless ways to get involved and make a difference.
